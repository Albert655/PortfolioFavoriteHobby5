About

My favorite hobby is playing basketball at my backyard. When I have enough time, I would play basketball. I can shoot hoops at the freethrow range of two points, I can do some trickshots, and I can dribble the ball. I had seen lots of people do trickshots. Also, I can get fresh air when I play basketball. I really like to do the dribbling and I had played basketball on my basketball class since I was in third grade. I actually played some mini-games of basket ball that my basketball teacher know such as "Horse". The goal is that you have to be the last standing person to win. If you miss five times and get the letters that spell HORSE, you lose. Even I liked the warm-ups. The warm-ups made me feel great.


I started playing basketball is when I joined the first basketball class that my mom registered me into.I met my friends that I met in the class. I enjoyed the minigames, the rules, and I even knew how to play the real basketball game.

My Favorite Minigames

My favorite minigames of basket ball is "Horse". I played this the first time since I was in third grade. This game is all about being the last one standing. If you are the last one standing, you win. If you throw the basketball for five attempts (five attempts of not making into the basket) and spell "HORSE", you lose.
